Our Mission & Identity

InnoVibe Care.EV Mobility, headquartered in Gitam ITBI, Rushikonda, Visakhapatnam, is designed as a software-as-infrastructure bridge between EV original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and electric vehicle owners.

Recognizing the massive gap in certified EV mechanics and dedicated diagnostic gear across India, we have built a centralized ecosystem to ensure reliability, standard pricing, and zero roadside anxiety.
